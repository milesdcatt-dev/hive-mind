## General Design Preferences

User prefers:
- Clean but visually interesting
- Less text
- Bigger buttons
- Better spacing
- Realistic not AI-looking
- Natural lighting
- Slightly imperfect realism
- Premium feel without being corporate
- Strong mobile layouts
- Visual proof over long copy
- PNG assets that can be layered into websites

Avoid:
- Too much text
- Overly polished AI photos
- Random design clutter
- Generic corporate wording
- Tiny buttons
- Weak CTAs
- Bad mobile spacing
- Fake-looking people
- Overuse of bright colors
- Designs that feel disconnected section to section
